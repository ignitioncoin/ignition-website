About Ignition Coin

A powerful, community driven, Dual Hybrid Cryptocurrency

WE HAVE A VISION

For creating a better blockchain

Masternodes and staking represent a new layer of security that work in highly secure clusters to provide a variety of decentralized services while eliminating the threat of low-cost network attacks.

WE INNOVATE

Dual Hybrid Security

At Ignition’s core is a unique fully-incentivized peer-to-peer network. Users are rewarded for securing the blockchain and masternodes are rewarded for validating, storing and serving the blockchain to users.
