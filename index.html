REWARDS

Be rewarded for helping secure the Ignition network, the following block reward will be deposited in your wallet after each block...

MASTERNODE

25% of all network rewards go to Master Nodes. Master Nodes run full nodes, like stakers, but they hold a collateral value of 3000IC in order to process anonymous and instant transactions for the network.

STAKING

25% of all network rewards go to Stakers. Stakers run a full node, and hold any amount of coins in their balance for at least 30 minutes to begin competing for new block rewards. Stakers secure the network health by contributing to the distribution of the blockchain efficiently.
